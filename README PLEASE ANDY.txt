Hi Andy, here are our controls for ShadowWizardMoneyGang 

Move: 
- WASD/Arrows (Keyboard)
- Left stick (Gamepad)

Look around:
- Delta (Mouse)
- Right stick (Gamepad)

Jump 
- Space (Keyboard) 
- Button South (Gamepad)

Laser
- Left Button (Mouse)
- Right trigger (Gamepad)

Pick Up 
- E (Keyboard)
- Button west (Gamepad)

Crouch 
- Control (Keyboard)
- Button east (Gamepad)

Sprint
- Shift (Keyboard)
- Right shoulder (Gamepad)
